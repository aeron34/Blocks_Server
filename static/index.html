Welcome to LINX the puzzle game!
This is a new mulitplayer puzzle game that
you can play with your friends or other people.

The controls are simple, WASD for movement.
SPACE to switch 2 blocks underneath you
"J" to switch 2 blocks vertically that your in front of
"I" to explode a link in front of you
"L" to explode a link underneath you
Have fun!
