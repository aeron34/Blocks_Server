What is LINX?

LINX is a Puzzle game in which you compete against others online for the highest score by stacking, rearrange, and swapping blocks. In this game there are 2 game modes, Free For All, and Friend Match. Both of these require you to get the highest score out of the others you're playing against to win the match

Controls

WASD - Movement

Space - Horizontal Swap

J - Vertical Swap

K - Depends On Character

L - Detonate Link beside you

I - Detonate Link underneath you

U - Depends On Character

O - Depends On Character

; - If Gizmo is Chosen, Detonates single block

Fundamentals

How you gain points in this game is by detonating Links. What is a Link? A Link is formed when 3 or more blocks are touching each other.

The easiest way to form Links is by Swapping

We'll cover Horizontal Swap first.

Stand on top of a block and press space. The block your standing over will be swapped with the block to it's left, ALWAYS LEFT, no matter what direction your facing.

The formula for how many points you get from detonating a Link:

size_of_link to the 5th power = points,

for example, a link of 4 is 4 ^ 5 = 1024.

- Grenades

Boxer

Then there is the Boxer character. He can do the same things as Gizmo except for distance swapping and using weapons. But he is faster than Gizmo, can jump higher, can explode blocks in place, and more.

You can press "O" beside a block to punch and explode it.

You can hold "S" and press "O" to explode a block underneath you.

Near a block, holding "A"(left) or "D"(right) and pressing "K" will perform a push punch, pushing the block a distance of 3 blocks.

Holding no direction will simply push it one block distance.

The Boxer, like Gizmo, has tools apart from basic mechanics that help you score higher than other players. He has moves that be utilised via button inputs. These work like they do in fighting games. Here are the input combinations, keep in mind the direction your facing does matter, which is why the facing right/left is there:

UPPERCUT - FACING RIGHT: "S", "D", "U"/FACING LEFT: "S", "A", "U"
OVERHAND (Past dark marker) - FACING RIGHT: "S", "D", "S", "D", "U",
FACING LEFT: "S", "A", "S", "A", "U"
